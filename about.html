About Me
I am a fourth-year undergraduate at the University of California, Berkeley within the College of Environmental Design studying Sustainable Environmental Design with an emphasis in city planning and geographical information systems (GIS).
Additionally, I work as an undergraduate student research assistant for the Center for Cities and School (CC+S) within the Department of City and Regional Planning focusing on the impacts of McKinney-Vento Education policies through housing, food security, transportation, while also designing and mapping visual ESRI StoryMaps.
I am passionate about advocating for resiliency within our built environment, especially concerning sustainable transportation.
